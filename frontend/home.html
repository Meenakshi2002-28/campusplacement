ABOUT US

Welcome to Lavoro, the official campus recruitment platform for the students of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Kochi Campus. Our mission is to bridge the gap between students and career opportunities by providing a streamlined and user-friendly platform to support every step of the recruitment journey. At Lavoro, we believe that every student should have easy access to the best career opportunities that match their skills and aspirations. Lavoro enables students to browse, search, and apply for job openings in a centralized, organized manner. By simplifying the recruitment process, we aim to make it easier for students to take charge of their career paths.
